# E02-01_0035-Autonomics-Email-Intro

<snippet id="E02-01_0035-Autonomics-Email-Intro_snippet">



The Email action lets you add the ability to send and receive emails in an automation, so you can create more complex workflows. By adding email capability, you can expand the features and data available to an automation.

The Email action is the intermediate step in your automation workflow. It's executed as part of the main process, allowing Autonomics to contact staff as needed.
